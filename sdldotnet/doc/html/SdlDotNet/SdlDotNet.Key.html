Key Enumeration

What we really want is a mapping of every raw key on the keyboard. To support international keyboards, we use the range 0xA1 - 0xFF as international virtual keycodes. We'll follow in the footsteps of X11... The keyboard syms have been cleverly chosen to map to ASCII
